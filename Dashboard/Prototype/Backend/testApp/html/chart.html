Contract Overview
The Perceptive Reach development and field pilot proposes to combine technology, outreach and clinical support to realize a clinically based data-driven early intervention and treatment solution aimed at suicide prevention. PwC will deliver a solution for analyzing multiple and integrated data sets with cutting-edge data analytic techniques and visualizations to identify at-risk individuals and populations and provide proactive and secure notifications of these results to Veteran support services. The Perceptive Reach project proposes to expand the capabilities of the Suicide Data Repository to include new interfaces to clinical data sources, integrated data analytics capabilities, a surveillance dashboard, and secure messaging "-" thus enhancing SDR capabilities to include upstream suicide intervention.
