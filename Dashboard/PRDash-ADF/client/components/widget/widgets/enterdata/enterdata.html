Initiation Checklist- To be completed by site facilitator(s)
1. Identified a primary patient provider.
2. Notified provider of the specific patient and program requirements
3. Asked provider to review treatment plans for the patient.
Outreach Checklist- To be completed by patient provider(or facilitator with provider approval)
4.Received notification from Site Facilitator about the patient
5. Reviewed current diagnoses and treatments
6. Established contact with the patient to review current diagnoses, symptoms, adherence and problems
Care Evaluation Checklist – To Be Completed by Patient Provider (or Facilitator with Provider Approval)
7.Updated the plan for management and treatment as appropriate
8. Evaluate appropriateness of Caring Communications program
9. Evaluate appropriateness of Safety Planning
Reasons for not Receiving VA Services– To Be Completed by Patient Provider (or Facilitator with Provider Approval)
10. Deceased
11. Cannot Contact
12. Refused Services
13. Care from community provider
14. Clinically not at risk
15. Other
